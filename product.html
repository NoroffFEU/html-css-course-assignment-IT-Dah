Whisper Jacket

The Whisper Jacket is a waterproof 3-layered shell jacket with fully taped seams. With a hood that is helmet-compatible, multiple pockets, including a pocket for ski-pass, and a zippered inner pocket, this jacket allows you to push your comfort zone. It has a two-way ventilation zipper under both arms, and ventilating mesh at the chest pockets. Get a customized fit and maximum comfort with the adjustable cuffs, together with the adjustable hem with drawsting.
